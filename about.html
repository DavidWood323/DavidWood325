About Me

Dave Wood

Started my oilfield career in the North Sea 1988.

Took a posting to the Far East in 1990 then later relocated from UK to Kota Kinabalu in Malaysia in 1996.


Returned to the UK in 2014 when my elder son started university , both boys had been in International School for

their education overseas . Relocated to Cambridge with the younger boy at Cambridge International school in

order to continue Cambridge International examinations in sync with his curriculum in Malaysia .


Both boys now finished university one taking Mechanical Engineering the other Aerospace Engineering .



Otherwise


Was not working during covid since the oil industry had significantly cut back on drilling .

Decided to make a return to the oilfield Nov 2022 , nicely got my old job back in Thailand & then promptly

failed the offshore medical in Bangkok . A stress test ECG produced an equivocal result and no more offshore job .


Returned to Kota Kinabalu for two months awaiting a heart scan in Kuala Lumpur.

Jan 2023 , heart scan all good but enough to hang up my rig boots if I can't pass the stress ECG.



These days fortunately my bills are not too much , and after a somewhat busy global career

generally seeking a role in a remote capacity .

Currently living in North Ireland .
