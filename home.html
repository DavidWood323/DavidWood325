The Data Migration position




Systems access and data handling I should be confident to manage .

A career requisite has always been absolute attention to detail .

And provision of quality data .

Repetitive data processing , I have had to do that at times within the earlier MWD/LWD framework . An occasional tool problem was the failure of a downhole memory data sync with surface data due to a data-time stamp error on the processor chip . The oil well formation memory data was at significantly higher resolution to real time data transmitted to surface . Entailed manually inputting a date-time stamp in hexadecimal format onto the chip for every data point . (With a significant amount of surface data on record (weight on bit log , rate of penetration log , pumps pressure) there was a way to do this accurately as well as comparison to the real time data log ).

A second occasional repetitive data intense role within the MWD/LWD environment . On occasion the surface systems could not decode real time data while drilling if encountering high noise to signal ratio . Entailed manually hand decoding real time signals (in hexadecimal format) into data , then manually inputting the data into computer systems for real time display to drilling rig floor and other locations . Not often but I have done that non stop every minute a 12 hour shift for days to keep the rig drilling to end of section . (On these occasions quality of hand decoded data was checked when memory data retrieved from tool).
